Professional Experience

A journey of impact across continents

  • Situation: Canton Schwyz education department rejected a private primary school permit application (13–22 pupils).
  • Task: Engaged as independent external strategic advisor to diagnose compliance failures and chart a resubmission path.
  • Action: Conducted systematic compliance analysis against Art. 62 Federal Constitution, Lehrplan 21, HarmoS Concordat, and Volksschulgesetz Schwyz; identified 13 specific gaps across curriculum, governance, qualifications, safeguarding, and data protection.
  • Result: Delivered a 9-page advisory report with a prioritised recommendation framework and 5-step resubmission action plan, equipping the founding team to approach Canton Schwyz as a credible, compliant operator.
  • Situation: Swiss schools sought meaningful, measurable partnerships with East African counterparts.
  • Task: Coordinate bilateral programs and ensure accountability for youth-leadership outcomes.
  • Action: Designed and evaluated workshops across public and private schools; led project planning, monitoring, and performance reporting; facilitated team-building and experiential learning programs.
  • Result: Strengthened Switzerland–East Africa intercultural collaboration and measurably improved program delivery outcomes.
  • Situation: Refugees and migrants faced systemic barriers to professional integration in Switzerland.
  • Task: Design and deliver targeted workshops to accelerate professional development.
  • Action: Led project management and entrepreneurship workshops; developed training curricula; mentored participants one-on-one.
  • Result: Strengthened professional and life skills for individuals with refugee and migrant backgrounds, supporting successful labour-market integration.
  • Situation: Deloitte ran an integration programme embedding international professionals in its consulting practice.
  • Task: Complete intensive rotations across financial consulting, taxation, legal services, and development research.
  • Action: Contributed analytical insights to refugee integration research and development initiatives across practice areas.
  • Result: Delivered actionable research findings that informed programme strategy improvements.
  • Situation: Community health projects lacked robust M&E systems and trained local facilitators.
  • Task: Manage M&E activities and build grassroots health and environmental management capacity.
  • Action: Trained refugee and cultural leaders in public health; conducted national assessments and health camps; provided strategic recommendations for programme improvement.
  • Result: Improved community-level M&E uptake and contributed evidence-based recommendations adopted in programme redesign.
  • Situation: The Ministry lacked standardised M&E infrastructure and data quality systems across its health facilities.
  • Task: Design and institutionalise a national M&E framework covering 50+ military health facilities.

  • Situation: Agricultural communities required mobilisation support and structured program management to access USAID-funded initiatives.
  • Task: Facilitate workshops and field coordination for agricultural and social development programmes.
  • Action: Delivered community mobilisation workshops; supported planning, reporting, and field coordination across multiple districts.
  • Result: Improved community participation rates and strengthened program reporting quality for USAID donor requirements.

  • Task: Manage financial reconciliation, cash flow, and monthly accounting reports in compliance with donor requirements.
  • Action: Maintained accurate financial records and ensured full compliance with organisational and donor financial procedures.
